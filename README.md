# MEDSAFE — Clinical Monitoring Platform

MEDSAFE is a modern hospital monitoring dashboard designed to help healthcare professionals manage patients, monitor ICU vitals, and respond to clinical alerts efficiently.

The system provides a clean interface for hospitals to track patient health conditions, monitor vital signs, and manage medical alerts in real time.

---

# Why MEDSAFE?

Hospitals require fast and reliable systems to monitor patient conditions.

MEDSAFE enables healthcare staff to:

• Track patient medical data  
• Monitor ICU vital signs  
• Detect critical risk alerts  
• Manage patient records efficiently  

---

# Key Features

## Patient Management

• Register new patients  
• Edit patient details  
• Delete patient records  
• Department classification  
• Risk level monitoring  

---

## ICU Monitoring System

Vitals monitored:

❤️ Heart Rate  
🫁 Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)  
💉 Blood Pressure  

Risk Levels:

🟢 Low  
🟡 Medium  
🟠 High  
🔴 Critical

## Clinical Alerts

• Automatic alert generation  
• Alert severity classification  
• Resolve alerts workflow  
• Active vs Resolved alerts

• Patient risk distribution
